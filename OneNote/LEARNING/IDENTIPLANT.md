**Danish Scurvy Grass**
 
- Pedicels ~3mm
- 4 sepals
- 4 white oval petals 2-3mm
- 6 stamens
- 1 pistil
- Fr spherical ovate made up of 2 capsules
- Flowers held in raceme with tight top, looking similar to a clover sometimes
 
- Tufted habit, Gregarious on roadside
- Few basal lvs, most on stem
- Palmately lobed, with 5 pointed lobes (ivy-like)
- Cordate
- Petioles of basal leaves v long, e.g. 30mm, stem lvs sessile
- Veins inset, only up to 2nd order, with no loops
- Light green hydathodes at tips of lobes
- Lower lf colour looking similar to e.g. Arum maculatum with thin pale film - Whole plant hairless & shiny
