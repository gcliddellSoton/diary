**SCANNING**  
Try looking at the results and making your own conclusions first BEFORE reading the authors' interpretation.
 
1. Prepare yourself before reading
2. Read looking specifically to answer the following questions:
    1. What was the problem/question
    2. What results did the authors find/claim
    3. What do you believe it means
    4. What do the authors believe it means
3. Do you need to look at this NOW. Do you need it in the future?
4. Make notes & continue to next step e.g. 1) deeper read; 2) file for later; 3) throw away
